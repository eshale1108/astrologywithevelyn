Welcome to Astrology with Evelyn

Hello, I’m Evelyn Hale. I’m excited to share my journey and the transformative work I offer as an astrologer.

Astrology is a powerful tool for understanding ourselves and the world around us. It allows us to weave a rich tapestry of personal insight through birth charts, offering guidance and hope for both the present and future. Since the age of 15, I have immersed myself in astrology, and my recent training through Rising Woman's Mystic Astrology Apprenticeship program has deepened my commitment to helping others. My focus is on supporting survivors of childhood trauma, helping them reconnect with their inner wisdom and break free from limiting beliefs.

My journey to healing began in childhood, navigating trauma and abuse, and led me to holistic practices that reversed self-sabotage and limiting beliefs. I believe that astrology can end cycles of generational trauma and contribute to collective healing. If my story resonates with you, I invite you to explore astrology as a tool for your personal growth and healing.

Offerings

Astrology Parties

Host a memorable astrology party where I provide personalized insights and readings in a group setting. Ideal for celebrations, get-togethers, or learning groups, I will help plan a custom event for 2-6 people.

Event options include:

  • Astrology Intro: An overview of Planets, Houses, and Signs.
  • Charts Review: Personal chart insights and interpretations.
  • Timing Info: Understanding astrological events and cycles.
  • Relationships Review: Exploring dynamics between groups or couples.
  • Q&A
  • Tarot/Oracle Readings

Send me birth info for attendees in advance for custom analysis. Each attendee will receive a color printout (or PDF) of their chart, with books, tarot cards, and other materials provided.

Events can be conducted live (Binghamton NY area) or virtually.

Pricing: $50 per person
Length: 1-2 hours
Group Size: 2-6 people

Personal Astrology Consultations

In a 60-minute session, we will analyze your astrological charts to uncover insights about current and upcoming planetary influences. My approach blends intuition with depth psychology, viewed through a trauma-informed lens.

In our session, we will:

  • Explore current astrological transits affecting your natal chart.
  • Anticipate potential events and transitions.
  • Identify ideal timing for significant life changes.
  • Understand your unique Divine Timing and cultivate trust in your journey.

We can also explore:

  • Optimal timing for business ventures or career changes.
  • Potential soulmate or life partner encounters.
  • Fertility windows and family planning.
  • Shifts in living arrangements or relocations.
  • Opportunities for travel and exploration.

Note: Sessions will not:

  • Dictate your choices or actions.
  • Override Divine Timing.
  • Foster unrealistic expectations about major life events.
  • Predict specific life changes or relationship longevity.
